Season 1(Tanjiro Kamado, Unwavering Resolve Arc)

Episode 1:The Cruelty

Tanjiro Kamado lives a quiet, peaceful life with his family in the snowy mountains of Japan, providing for them by selling charcoal at the nearby town. After returning home from town, Tanjiro finds his family brutally murdered by a demon, with only his sister Nezuko surviving—though she’s been turned into a demon herself. Despite her transformation, Nezuko shows signs of retaining her humanity, which catches the attention of Giyu Tomioka.

Episode 2:Trainer Sakonji Urokodaki

Following Giyu’s guidance, Tanjiro travels with Nezuko to Mount Sagiri to meet Sakonji Urokodaki, a former member of the Demon Slayer Corps. Along the way, he faces a demon and uses his wits and determination to defeat it—his first taste of the dangers ahead. Urokodaki tests Tanjiro’s resolve by making him survive a perilous descent through the mountain filled with traps. When Tanjiro passes the test, Urokodaki agrees to train him in the art of becoming a Demon Slayer.

Episode 3:Sabito and Makomo

Tanjiro undergoes harsh training under Urokodaki, learning sword techniques and mastering Total Concentration Breathing. As the months pass, his physical abilities grow—but his final challenge is to split a massive boulder. Struggling to do so, Tanjiro meets two mysterious children named Sabito and Makomo, former students of Urokodaki. They push him beyond his limits, especially Sabito, who mercilessly trains him in combat. Through their guidance, Tanjiro eventually achieves the impossible—he cleaves the boulder in two, earning the right to take part in the Final Selection.

Episode 4:Final Selection

Tanjiro arrives at a remote forest where the Demon Slayer Corps holds the Final Selection—an intense survival test where participants must last seven days among bloodthirsty demons. Amidst the fight for survival, Tanjiro encounters the powerful and grotesque Hand Demon, infamous for killing many of Urokodaki’s previous pupils. Driven by sorrow and vengeance, Tanjiro uses his Water Breathing techniques to defeat the demon in a fierce battle. He survives the ordeal and is officially accepted into the Demon Slayer Corps, ready to face a dark and dangerous world ahead.

Episode 5:My Own Steel

Tanjiro passes the Final Selection and chooses the ore for his special sword. He returns to Urokodaki’s home, where Nezuko finally wakes up after a long rest. Tanjiro receives his black Nichirin blade and learns that some demons have unique Blood Demon Arts. His crow delivers his first mission: investigate mysterious disappearances of young girls in a nearby town.

Episode 6:Swordsman Accompanying a Demon

Tanjiro sets out on his first mission as a Demon Slayer, traveling to a town where young girls have mysteriously vanished. He meets Kazumi, whose fiancée was one of the victims, and begins investigating using his keen sense of smell. At night, Tanjiro detects a demon and rescues a girl from its swamp-like portal. He discovers the demon can split into three clones and move through underground puddles. As the battle intensifies, Nezuko bursts from her box and joins the fight, protecting the humans. Tanjiro realizes these demons may hold clues about Muzan Kibutsuji—the original demon who turned Nezuko—and prepares to confront them.

Episode 7:Muzan Kibutsuji

Tanjiro dives into the demon’s swamp dimension to finish off the remaining clones, using his Water Breathing Sixth Form: Whirlpool to defeat them underwater. Meanwhile, Nezuko battles the last clone on the surface, showing surprising strength. After Tanjiro returns and finishes the fight, he questions the demon about Muzan Kibutsuji—but the demon is too terrified to speak his name and is swiftly killed. Tanjiro comforts Kazumi, the grieving fiancé, and prepares to move on. His crow delivers a new mission: head to Asakusa, Tokyo. Upon arriving, Tanjiro is overwhelmed by the bustling city—but then catches a familiar scent. He tracks it and comes face-to-face with Muzan himself, disguised as a human with a wife and child. Muzan, sensing danger, turns a passerby into a demon to distract Tanjiro and escape.
